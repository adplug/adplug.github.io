Herbulot AdLib (HERAD) System was created by Remi Herbulot, one of Cryo Interactive Entertainment co-founders and lead programmers. Stephane Picq, Cryo's first music composer also helped to design HERAD.

Music files in this format usually packed either with HSQ or SQX compression algorithms and have appropriate extensions.

SDB stands for Sound Blaster. This format is designed to be played on OPL2-compatible sound chip. HERAD SDB format is used in Dune and KGB games. HERAD SDB version 2 format is used only in MegaRace game.

The archive contains sound drivers for playback and both packed and unpacked test music files. Also it includes MegaRace (HERAD v2) music player by Jepael.
