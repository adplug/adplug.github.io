God of Thunder stores its music in this format. It is a raw AdLib format, containing the exact register and data pairs to send to the OPL chip.

Songs are played at a different tempo (i.e. each delay tick lasts a different length of time): menu.got plays at 140 Hz, all other songs plays at 120 Hz.

Format documentation is available here.
