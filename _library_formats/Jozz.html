--- filename: Jozz.zip flags: " PM" extension: .SNG desc: Jozz Adlib Music Driver ---

JCH sent me this wonderful archive of adlib music made by Johannes Bjerregaard (Maniacs of Noise at that time) around 1992. The music was written in his own driver routine. A (somewhat buggy) player is included in the archive. Nothing more is known about the internals of the format. I wish someone would figure it out since the music is really wonderful!
